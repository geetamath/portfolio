About Me

I am a SAP Analyst, AI Engineer and Robotics enthusiast, currently pursuing my M.Tech in Robotics & Artificial Intelligence at M.S. Ramaiah Institute of Technology, Bangalore, with a CGPA of 8.95/10. I sincerely enjoy working at the intersection of deep learning, computer vision, and autonomous systems, and I strive every day to grow both as a researcher and as a professional.

My most recent research was carried out at CMTI (Central Manufacturing Technology Institute), Bangalore, where I had the privilege of working alongside government scientists to develop a deep learning-based drone detection and tracking system using YOLOv8. It was a deeply rewarding experience that helped me understand how academic AI research can be meaningfully applied in real-world, industrial settings.

Before beginning my master's, I had the opportunity to work for 1.5 years at Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) as an SAP Basis Analyst, managing cloud infrastructure and enterprise systems. That experience taught me the value of reliability, teamwork, and attention to detail — qualities I now carry into my AI and robotics work with great appreciation.
